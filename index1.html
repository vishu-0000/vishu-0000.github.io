About Vijyoti

Vijyoti, focused on healthcare and marketing communications and media services sector, was founded in 2007. Headquartered in Delhi, NCR with representative offices in Mumbai (India) and Montreal (Canada), Vijyoti launched with its Marketing Communications and Media line of business, predominantly offering video production and editing services. Serving diverse industries, and supporting both in-house marketing and HR departments, Vijyoti established itself, being sought for its unique blend of creative ideas, screenplay and production value.


In 2014, Vijyoti, diversified into the healthcare sector. Leveraging its core expertise in marketing communications, Vijyoti launched its second line of business - branding, distribution and sales of point of care medical diagnostic equipment and reagents through its agreement with Tem International GmbH, a Munich based international sales and distribution company. TEM International is one of the two entities of TEM Group, an international medical technology company headquartered in Basel, Switzerland. The second entity, Tem Innovations GmbH, deals with the development and production of ROTEM® point-of-care diagnostic systems and reagents. Vijyoti is the Indian distributor for ROTEM® point-of-care diagnostic systems. Besides this Vijyoti is the Channel partner of Werfen India Pvt Ltd a diagnostic solution for life. Vijyoti is aggressively marketing the Arterial Blood Gas Analyzers of Werfen called Gem series in corporate and Govt Hospitals in India.
